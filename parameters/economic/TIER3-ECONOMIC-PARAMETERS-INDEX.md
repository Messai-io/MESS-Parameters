# Tier 3 Economic Parameters Documentation Index

## Overview

This directory contains comprehensive documentation for 30 specialized economic
parameters critical for MES system evaluation and investment decision-making.
Each parameter includes 5000-7000 words of detailed analysis covering economic
theory, cost modeling, financial analysis, market assessment, risk evaluation,
and practical applications.

## Documentation Standards

Each parameter documentation includes:

- **Comprehensive Overview**: 500-700 words introducing the parameter
- **Economic Theory and Frameworks**: 2000-2500 words on theoretical foundations
- **Cost Modeling Methodologies**: 1500-2000 words on modeling approaches
- **Financial Analysis Techniques**: 1000-1500 words on financial evaluation
- **Market Assessment**: 1000-1500 words on market conditions
- **Risk Evaluation**: 800-1200 words on risk factors
- **Investment Decision Criteria**: 800-1200 words on decision metrics
- **Case Studies and Benchmarks**: 1000-1500 words on real-world examples
- **Literature References**: 25-30 academic and industry citations
- **Practical Applications**: 400-600 words on implementation guidance

- Capital Cost: <$5,000/kW for MFC
- Operating Cost: <$0.30/m³ for wastewater
- Payback Period: <7 years
- IRR: >10%

- Cost per kg H₂: <$3 for MEC

### Long-term (2030+)

- Positive NPV without subsidies
- TCO competitive advantage
- Mainstream commercial deployment
