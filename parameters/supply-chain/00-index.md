## Vendor Qualification Criteria

### Tier 1 Suppliers (Critical Components)

- ISO 9001 certification required
- 99% on-time delivery
- <100 ppm defect rate
- 24/7 technical support
- Innovation partnership

### Tier 2 Suppliers (Important Components)

- Quality system in place
- 95% on-time delivery
- <500 ppm defect rate
- Business hours support
- Cost optimization focus

### Tier 3 Suppliers (Commodity Items)

- Basic quality controls
- 90% on-time delivery
- <1000 ppm defect rate
- Standard support
- Price competitiveness
