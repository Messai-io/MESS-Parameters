## Implementation Guidelines

### Material Selection Criteria

1. **Application Requirements**

   - Power/current density needs
   - Environmental conditions
   - Operational lifetime
   - Cost constraints

2. **Biocompatibility Assessment**

   - Cell viability impacts
   - Biofilm formation support
   - Long-term stability
   - Toxicity considerations

3. **System Integration**
   - Compatibility with existing components
   - Scalability potential
   - Manufacturing feasibility
   - Maintenance requirements

### Best Practices

1. **Start with proven materials** (CNTs, graphene, metal NPs)
2. **Optimize for specific applications** rather than universal solutions
3. **Consider hybrid approaches** combining multiple materials
4. **Prioritize scalability** from the design phase
5. **Implement quality control** at all stages
