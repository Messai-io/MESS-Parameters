# Environmental Parameters Documentation Index

## Overview

This comprehensive environmental parameters documentation provides detailed
analysis of 30 critical environmental parameters for Microbial Electrochemical
Systems (MES). Each parameter document contains 5000-7000 words of in-depth
technical content covering measurement methodologies, system-specific
considerations, regulatory requirements, and optimization strategies.

## Parameter Categories

### 🌍 Environmental Impact Parameters (10 parameters)

Assessment of MES effects on environmental quality and ecosystem health.

1. **[Carbon Footprint Assessment](./impact/carbon-footprint-assessment.md)**
   🟢🟡🟣🔴

   - Comprehensive GHG emissions quantification throughout system lifecycle
   - Units: kg CO₂e/kWh, t CO₂e/year
   - Typical range: 0.1-0.5 kg CO₂e/kWh for optimized systems
   - Key focus: Life cycle assessment, carbon neutrality pathways

2. **[Life Cycle Environmental Impact](./impact/life-cycle-environmental-impact.md)**
   🟢🟡🟣🔴

   - Multi-criteria environmental burden assessment from cradle to grave
   - Units: ReCiPe points (Pt), mPt/kWh
   - Typical range: 15-60 mPt/kWh for industrial scale
   - Key focus: 18 midpoint categories, ecosystem quality endpoints

3. **[Water Quality Impact](./impact/water-quality-impact.md)** 🟢🟡🟣🔴

   - Effects on aquatic ecosystems and water resource quality
   - Units: Water Quality Index (0-100), mg/L reduction
   - Typical improvement: 20-40 WQI points
   - Key focus: Physical, chemical, biological parameters

4. **[Air Quality Impact](./impact/air-quality-impact.md)** 🟢🟡🟣🔴

   - Atmospheric emissions and air pollutant management
   - Units: AQI points, kg pollutant/year
   - Typical reduction: 80-95% VOCs vs conventional
   - Key focus: GHGs, criteria pollutants, bioaerosols

5. **[Soil Contamination Assessment](./impact/soil-contamination-assessment.md)**
   🟢🟡🟣🔴

   - Soil quality effects from operations and biosolids application
   - Units: mg/kg dry weight, % reduction
   - Typical performance: 70-95% heavy metal reduction
   - Key focus: Contamination prevention, remediation capability

6. **[Ecosystem Impact Analysis](./impact/ecosystem-impact-analysis.md)**
   🟢🟡🟣🔴

   - Biodiversity and ecosystem function assessment
   - Units: Shannon index, Ecosystem Integrity (0-100)
   - Typical change: +15-25% species richness
   - Key focus: Habitat quality, ecosystem services

7. **[Biodiversity Impact Assessment](./impact/biodiversity-impact-assessment.md)**
   🟢🟡🟣🔴

   - Species diversity and genetic resource effects
   - Units: Species richness, Simpson's diversity index
   - Key focus: Conservation strategies, habitat enhancement

8. **[Noise Pollution Assessment](./impact/noise-pollution-assessment.md)**
   🟢🟡🟣🔴

   - Acoustic emissions and sound level management
   - Units: dB(A), Leq
   - Typical levels: < 55 dB(A) at property line
   - Key focus: Quiet operation advantage

9. **[Visual Impact Assessment](./impact/visual-impact-assessment.md)** 🟢🟡🟣🔴

   - Landscape and aesthetic considerations
   - Units: Visual Impact Rating (1-10)
   - Key focus: Green infrastructure integration

10. **[Land Use Impact](./impact/land-use-impact.md)** 🟢🟡🟣🔴
    - Spatial footprint and land transformation effects
    - Units: m²/PE, hectares
    - Typical reduction: 40-60% vs conventional
    - Key focus: Compact design, multi-use integration

### ♻️ Sustainability Metrics Parameters (10 parameters)

Quantification of sustainability performance and circular economy contributions.

11. **[Resource Efficiency Metrics](./sustainability/resource-efficiency-metrics.md)**
    🟢🟡🟣🔴

    - Units: kg output/kg input, efficiency ratios
    - Key focus: Circular economy principles

12. **[Energy Return on Investment](./sustainability/energy-return-on-investment.md)**
    🟢🟡🟣🔴

    - Units: EROI ratio (dimensionless)
    - Typical range: 2-5 for MFC systems
    - Key focus: Energy payback time

13. **[Water Footprint Analysis](./sustainability/water-footprint-analysis.md)**
